New €1.5 million Zipit Donadea Forest Adventure Park Opens in Co. Kildare
Zipit Donadea, a new forest adventure park in Co. Kildare was officially opened today by Minister for Agriculture, Food and the Marine, Martin Heydon TD. The project was awarded €1.2 million in funding through Fáilte Ireland’s Regenerative Tourism and Placemaking Scheme, which is co-funded by the Government of Ireland and the European Union under the EU Just Transition Fund.
Work commences on Inis, a new indoor visitor experience on Oileán Cléire (Cape Clear)
Work has officially commenced on Inis, a new indoor all weather visitor attraction on Oileán Cléire (Cape Clear Island) off the coast of County Cork that will bring to life the island’s unique history, culture and maritime heritage.
